Shipping

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and overnight delivery is available for next-business-day arrival. Shipping times are calculated from the order confirmation date.

Returns & Refunds

We offer 30 days from the date of purchase to return items in original, unused condition. To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number.

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.

For defective or incorrect items, we provide a prepaid return label. For regular returns due to change of mind, return shipping is the customer's responsibility.
